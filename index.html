About Me

The journalist who stayed curious

I started my career writing scripts for TV newscasts — tracking down breaking stories, writing on deadline, and understanding exactly what makes an audience stop scrolling. That training never left.

Today I apply that same editorial lens to PR. I don't just pitch products; I find the news hook, craft the narrative, and give journalists a story they actually want to cover.

My background in journalism means I think the way reporters do — which is exactly why clients I work with get placed in the publications that matter.
